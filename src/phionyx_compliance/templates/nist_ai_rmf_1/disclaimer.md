**Framework-specific addendum (NIST AI RMF 1.0).** This report is structured against the four core functions (MAP, MEASURE, MANAGE, GOVERN) of NIST's Artificial Intelligence Risk Management Framework, version 1.0 (NIST AI 100-1, January 2023, DOI 10.6028/NIST.AI.100-1). The RMF is voluntary; NIST does not certify systems against it. This report does not establish "RMF compliance" — it provides runtime evidence the operator can use within an RMF-aligned risk management process. The official text: <https://doi.org/10.6028/NIST.AI.100-1>. Operator must consult the RMF's accompanying Playbook for category-specific guidance and refer to subsequent NIST AI profiles (e.g. NIST AI 600-1 GenAI profile) as applicable to their use case.
