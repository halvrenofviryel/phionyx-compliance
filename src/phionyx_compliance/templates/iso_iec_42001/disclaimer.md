**Framework-specific addendum (ISO/IEC 42001:2023).** This report is structured against a subset of ISO/IEC 42001:2023 (Artificial intelligence management system) clauses and Annex A controls. ISO/IEC 42001 is a *certifiable* standard — a notified body, not Phionyx, awards conformity assessment. This report is one input to an internal audit (Clause 9.2) or to the management review (Clause 9.3); it is NOT a substitute for either. The official text: <https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html>. Operator must engage a qualified ISO/IEC 42001 lead auditor for any conformity assessment. The Annex A controls covered here (A.4, A.6, A.8) are a subset; A.5 (impact assessment), A.7 (data management), A.9 (use), and A.10 (third-party relationships) require evidence outside the runtime chain.
