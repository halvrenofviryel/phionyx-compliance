**Framework-specific addendum (OWASP Agentic AI Threats v1.0).** This report is structured against the OWASP Foundation's *Agentic AI — Threats and Mitigations* document, Version 1.0 (February 2025), produced by the OWASP Generative AI Security Project / Top 10 for LLM Applications team. OWASP does not certify systems against this taxonomy — it is a community threat catalogue. The official document: <https://genai.owasp.org/resource/agentic-ai-threats-and-mitigations/>. Threat coverage statements in this report describe which threats the runtime evidence chain provides direct evidence against (T2, T6, T8) and which require operator controls outside the chain scope (T1 memory, T4 resource, T5 cascading effects). Operator must consult the OWASP document for the full threat descriptions, mitigation patterns, and ongoing taxonomy revisions.
